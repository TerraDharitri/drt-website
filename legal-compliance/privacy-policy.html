2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information You Provide to Us:

Contact Forms: When you contact us through forms on our Website (e.g., for general inquiries or partnership information), we collect the information you choose to provide. This may include your name, email address, and optionally, your phone number, along with the content of your message. These forms are processed on our behalf by Tally.so, a third-party service provider. We encourage you to review Tally.so's privacy policy for information on their data handling practices, available on their website.

Staking Platform (stake.dharitri.org): When you access our Staking Platform to stake pREWA tokens, we collect your wallet address to facilitate staking operations. Staking parameters (APY, lockup periods, minimum amounts) are displayed on the platform and sourced from smart contract configurations. All staking transactions are recorded on the Binance Smart Chain and become part of the public blockchain record that cannot be modified or deleted.

Information We Collect Automatically:

Website Visitors:

Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Website and hold certain information. For detailed information about the types of cookies we use (including strictly necessary, performance/analytics, and marketing/targeting cookies), the specific cookies deployed, their purposes, and how you can manage your cookie preferences, please refer to our separate Cookie Policy, which forms part of this Privacy Policy.

Log Data and Analytics: When you visit our Website, your browser automatically sends information that we, or our third-party analytics providers such as Google Analytics, collect. This information may include your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our Website that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data. This data is generally aggregated and used for analytical purposes to improve our Website.

Staking Platform Users:

  • Wallet Connection Data: When you connect your wallet to our Staking Platform, we temporarily collect your wallet address and may cache transaction history for display purposes.
  • Staking Preferences: Information about your staking choices, including amounts, duration, and reward preferences.
  • Smart Contract Interaction Logs: Technical data related to your interactions with our staking smart contracts.
Information Regarding pREWA Token:

The acquisition of pREWA tokens takes place on third-party decentralized exchanges, such as PancakeSwap. Dugong does not directly process these financial transactions or collect personal financial information related to such purchases through this Website. Your interactions with these third-party platforms, including any wallet addresses used, are subject to their terms and the public nature of the Binance Smart Chain.

Dugong does not currently conduct Know Your Customer (KYC) or Anti-Money Laundering (AML) checks or collect such personal information directly through this Website or Staking Platform for individuals interested in the pREWA token.

Future Platform Data (Forward-Looking Statement):

Please be aware that the future Dharitri platform, as outlined in our Project Roadmap, is designed to empower smallholder farmers. This will involve the collection by Dugong (or a future designated project entity like the Dharitri Foundation) of specific information from participating farmers and agents, such as Farmer IDs (including optional photos), farm activity data, conservation records, and potentially geospatial data. Sensitive data collected in the future will be subject to enhanced consent and security protocols, detailed in platform-specific notices. 5. Data Security and Blockchain Considerations

Dugong takes reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Breach Notification:

In the event of a data breach that poses risks to user rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and relevant regulatory authorities within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach (GDPR requirements) or 48 hours (Kenya DPA requirements), as applicable.

Blockchain and Cryptocurrency Specific Considerations:
  • Blockchain Transaction Permanence: Please be aware that blockchain transactions are permanent and publicly viewable. We cannot modify or delete transaction records once they are confirmed on the blockchain. Your wallet address and staking activities will be visible to anyone who can access blockchain explorers.
  • Smart Contract Security: While we implement security best practices in our smart contracts, the decentralized nature of blockchain technology means that smart contract interactions carry inherent risks that cannot be completely eliminated.
  • Private Key Responsibility: You are solely responsible for the security of your private keys and wallet access. We do not have access to your private keys and cannot recover lost access to your wallet.
  • Open Source Software: Our project utilizes open-source software under GPL v3 license, including our blockchain fork of MultiversX and newly developed code. While this doesn't directly affect personal data, users should be aware that project code will be publicly available for review and audit.
6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

  • Website Analytics Data: Information collected for website analytics (e.g., through Google Analytics) is retained for a standard period as configured within our analytics tools, or as long as necessary for analytical purposes to help us understand how visitors use our Website.
  • Contact Form Submissions: We will retain information submitted through our contact forms (including your name, email address, and any optional phone number or message you provide) for as long as necessary to address your inquiry and for a reasonable period thereafter for our internal record-keeping, typically not exceeding 24 months after the last communication, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
  • Staking Platform Data: Wallet addresses and staking preferences are retained as long as you maintain active stakes or for 12 months after your last staking activity, whichever is longer.
  • Blockchain Data: Data recorded on blockchain networks is permanent and cannot be deleted by us or any other party.
  • Cookie Data: The retention period for data collected via cookies varies depending on the type of cookie. Please see our Cookie Policy for more detailed information on cookie duration.

Once the retention period expires, personal data shall be appropriately disposed of or anonymized. The rights to access, erasure, rectification, and data portability cannot be enforced after the expiration of the relevant retention period if the data has been deleted. Note that blockchain-recorded data cannot be deleted regardless of retention periods.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location and applicable data protection laws, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These may include:

  • The right to access: You have the right to request copies of your personal information held by Dugong.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure (right to be forgotten): You have the right to request that we erase your personal information, under certain conditions. Please note that data recorded on blockchain networks cannot be erased.
  • The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal information, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal information, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions. Blockchain-recorded data may have limitations on portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent: If we are relying on your consent to process your personal information, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.

For EU Residents: GDPR rights apply to your personal data. You may contact us at privacy@dharitri.org for data protection inquiries.

Important Limitation: Please note that data recorded on blockchain networks (including staking transactions and wallet addresses) cannot be modified, deleted, or restricted by us or any other party due to the immutable nature of blockchain technology.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@dharitri.org. We may need to request specific information from you to help us confirm your identity before responding to such requests. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.
