2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

Cookies can be "session" cookies (which are deleted once you close your web browser) or "cookies that stay until you delete them" (also called "persistent" cookies). They can also be "first-party" cookies (set by the website you are visiting, i.e., by Dugong for the Dharitri Website) or "third-party" cookies (set by a domain other than the one you are visiting, e.g., by Google Analytics).

3. How and Why We Use Cookies

Dugong uses cookies on the Dharitri Website and Staking Platform for several reasons:

  • To ensure our Website and Staking Platform function correctly and securely
  • To understand how our services are used, which helps us improve performance and user experience
  • To remember your preferences (such as your cookie consent choices and wallet connections)
  • To remember your staking preferences and transaction history on our Staking Platform
  • To provide enhanced security through CSRF protection and session management

We use the following types of cookies on the Dharitri Website and Staking Platform:

Purpose: These cookies are essential for you to browse the Website and Staking Platform and use their core features, such as navigating between pages, managing your cookie consent preferences, connecting wallets, and submitting forms. 4.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

Purpose: These cookies collect information about how you use our Website and Staking Platform, such as which pages you visit most often, how much time you spend on those pages, staking activity patterns, and if you encounter any error messages. The information collected is typically aggregated and anonymous and helps us improve how our services work.

Google Analytics uses cookies to help us analyze how users use the Website and Staking Platform. The information generated by the cookie about your use of our services (including your IP address, which is anonymized) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers that may be located outside of your country of residence. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of our services, compiling reports on activity for us, and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage.

You can learn more about Google's practices by going to https://policies.google.com/privacy/partners/, and opt-out of them by downloading the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on, available at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Consent: We will only place these cookies on your device if you give us your consent through our cookie consent banner.

6. Your Choices and Managing Cookies

You have several options for controlling and managing cookies:

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our Website or Staking Platform, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. GDPR requires prior consent for non-essential cookies. Our banner enforces this and ensures you can accept or reject non-essential cookies with equal ease, and access to our Website is not blocked if you reject cookies.

This banner allows you to accept or reject the use of non-essential cookies (Performance/Analytics cookies). Your preferences will be remembered for future visits, but you can change them at any time through the consent management tool provided on our services.

Withdrawing Consent: To withdraw consent, use our cookie settings tool or clear your browser cookies. Withdrawing consent requires reloading the page. Essential cookies will reapply automatically to maintain basic functionality.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. You can usually find these settings in the "options" or "preferences" menu of your browser. You can set your browser to block certain cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent to your device. You can also delete cookies already stored on your device.

To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org.

Google Analytics Opt-out

As mentioned above, you can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Important Limitations

Please note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, some parts of our Website and Staking Platform may not function properly or may be inaccessible. Disabling strictly necessary cookies will likely impact functionality including:

  • Form submissions
  • Wallet connections on the Staking Platform
  • Security features
  • User preference settings
